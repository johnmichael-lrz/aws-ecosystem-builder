# aws-ecosystem-builder

This repository exists for anyone learning AWS who wants 
to see not just what to build, but why it works, where 
it breaks, and what it actually means for a real system.

Everything here is written the way I wish I had found it 
when I started.

---

## What Is Here

### /projects
Hands-on AWS builds with full architectural reasoning. 
Each project covers the business problem being solved, 
the design decisions behind it, and an honest account 
of what broke and how it was fixed.

### /workshops
Materials from AWS workshops I have facilitated — 
session structure, lab guides, and facilitator notes 
that anyone can pick up and use.

### /insights
Standalone notes on AWS concepts, service comparisons, 
and architectural patterns worth understanding before 
you encounter them in the console.

---

## How Everything Is Documented

Every project follows the same structure so it is easy 
to read and easy to replicate:

- **The Business Problem** — why this exists in the 
  real world
- **What I Built** — the exact components created
- **Design Decisions** — why specific choices were made
- **Friction Log** — what broke, the exact error, and 
  how it was resolved
- **Cost and Security Audit** — what it costs and how 
  it is locked down
