# AWS
One to two sentences in plain English. NO jargon without explanation. Write it the way you would explain it to someone intelligent who never heard of it.

## Why It Exists
What problem existed before this? What breaks or becomes impossible without it? This is the business reason, not the technical definition.

## How It Works
Explain the mechanics. How does data move through it? What are the core components and how do they relate to each other? Use physical analogies where the concept is abstract.

## Key Jargons
Define every piece of jargon above in plain language, in the order it first appeared.

## In my Own Words
Write a two or three sentence explanation of this entire concept explaining it to a workshop participant who just asked you what it is. NO notes. No looking back at the sections above. Just write it.

## What I Am Still Unclear About
Be honest. Write down what you read but could not yet explain confidently. This becomes your starting point. for the next study sessions.
