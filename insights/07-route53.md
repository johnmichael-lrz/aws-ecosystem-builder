## Why It Exists

## How It Works
Explain the mechanics. How does data move through it? What are the core components and how do they relate to each other? Use physical analogies where the concept is abstract.

## How It Connects to Other Services
No AWS service works in isolation. What does this service depend on? What depends on it? Draw the relationships explicitly.

## Why This and Not Something Else

## What Happens If It Fails

## How It Applies in the Console

## Key Jargons

## In My Own Words
Write a two or three sentence explanation of this entire concept as if you are explaining it to a workshop participant who just asked you what it is. No notes. No looking back at the sections above. Just write it.
